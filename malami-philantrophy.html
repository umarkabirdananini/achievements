PHILANTHROPIC ACTIVITIES/ACHIEVEMENTS


Nigeria’ North West is key to the improvement of the country’s development situation and attainment of sustainable development goals. The northwest has a population of more at least 50 million people and two-third of them are living in abject poverty and facing other challenges such as out-of-school children and limited access to quality education among others.

Sequel to the above prevailing circumstances, four (4) Non-Governmental organizations founded by one man and his two wives, Abubakar Malami, SAN, are working assiduously to ensure that happens.

Khadimiyya for Justice and Development Initiative
Kadi Malami Foundation
Aisha Abubakar Malami Center for Women Development (AAMCWD)
Al-Iman Charity Foundation
Including many of the Malami’s Support organizations.

6. WOMEN EMPOWERMENT WITH SUNDRY APPOINTMENTS

7. TOWARDS ENDING POVERTY, HUNGER AND ENSURING FOOD SECURITY
These activities have immensely contributed to the attainment of ten key areas of the SDGs as enumerated below:
1. Toward Ending poverty: The organizations goal of contributing to reducing poverty and hunger is done by providing income to households through its rice anchor borrower programme and the Casava programme. At least 1702 farmers benefited in the rice project and at least 2901 benefited from two cassava projects with all beneficiaries using the proceeds to provide sustenance, school fees and other needs of their families and thereby contributing to the reduction of poverty.
2. Ending hunger and ensuring food security with sustainable agriculture: The four organizations under the umbrella of Abubakar Malami have also contributed to ending hunger and ensuring sustainable agriculture by producing at least 100 tonnes of food commodities in its rice and cassava programmes. These have added to outputs of Kebbi state despite the flood experienced last year. With increased production of food the number of undernourished people has dropped in Kebbi state. Also the feeding programme under the Kadi Malami Foundation provides nutritious meals to 21 Islamic schools weekly thereby adding to the reduction of hunger and undernourishment.


8. ENSURING HEALTHY LIVING FOR ALL AGES, ENSURING INCLUSIVE, EQUITABLE AND QUALITY EDUCATION; ACHIEVE GENDER EQUALITY AND EMPOWER ALL WOMEN AND GIRLS
Ensuring healthy living for all ages: Still, the four organizations, in particular, Khadimaya runs a community health programme that provides access to professional health care to 1000 members of Nassarawa Ward A of Birnin Kebbi Local Government. The programme coordinated by Hussein Abdullahi has continued to ensure healthy living for the 1000 members of Nasarawa ward and their families. There is now a plan to expand the arrangement to at least one ward of each Local Government in Kebbi State.
Ensure inclusive, equitable and quality education: All four organizations have contributed to quality education more particularly the Kadi Malami Foundation which sponsors secondary and tertiary education. It had also provided support to educational pursuits through feeding of Islamic students.
Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls: Under two organizations, the Hajiya Aisha Women and Children Foundation and Al-Iman Charity Foundation the Abubakar Malami organizations have contributed immensely to women empowerment and gender equality. They have each trained more than 3000 women in various trades such as catering, cosmetic and tailoring and others. These have given them the valuable skills to earn a living and contribute to the provision of food and other needs for their respective families.


9. ENSURE EFFICIENCY IN WATER SUPPLY AND SANITATION & OTHERS
Ensure efficiency in water supply and sanitation: All the four organizations under the umbrella of Abubakar Malami are in the vital sector of provision of clean water and sanitation. All together they have helped sink 128 water Boreholes alongside electric generators. These have provided much-needed drinking water and improving access to clean water for drinking, hand washing and other household chores. The water programme has touched the lives of at least 60,000 households at 150 people to one borehole.
The activities of the four organizations under the umbrella of Abubakar Malami have also intermittently covered the following three SDG areas; Partnership by partnering with the CBN on rice and cassava programmes and similarly partnering with NALDA on goat rearing. The organizations also ensure the reduction of inequalities in income by improving people’ ability to meet their needs. It also takes action on combating climate change through speak up about its effects and reducing water waste.
