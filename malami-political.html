CONTRIBUTION TO DEMOCRACY IN NIGERIA

Malami is a politician and democrat portraying his strives as a politician in participation party politics serving through the ranks to the level of a national legal adviser, formation of the APC, participating in NEC/solving the party crisis.

This can be seen in his effort to establish Local Government autonomy/judiciary autonomy, following the passage of the EO10 and liaising with the Governor’s forum, and finally moving the supreme court to act on the same.

POLITICAL LANDSCAPE

Mr Malami, SAN, has long association with the political developments of the country. He served as a member of the Constitution Sub – Committee for All Progressives Congress (APC) 6th June 2014. He was Chairman, All Progressives Congress (APC) Kano State Congress Appeal Committee 15th May 2014. He also served as a member of the Kebbi State Committee on National Conference Convened by the Federal Government in 2013.

Another political assignment saw him serve as the member of the APC Electoral Committee for Anambra State Gubernatorial Election in 2013, as well as a member, National Convention and Congress Committee in 2013. This is in addition to being a member of the Merger Committees between the CPC, CAN & ANPP which led to the formation of the All Progressive Congress (APC) in 2013.

Prior to the formation of the APC, Mr Malami, SAN had served as the National Legal Adviser of the Congress for Progressive Change (CPC) 2011-2013; Chairman of the Legal Team of the CPC Presidential Campaign Organization, 2011; Member of the Kano State Local Government Election Tribunal in 2004; Resource Person on the Manifesto drafting Sub-Committee of Inter Joint Party; and also member of the PDP Legal Team for Election Petition in 2003.
