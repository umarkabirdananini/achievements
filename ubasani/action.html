THE UBA SANI ACTION PLAN

In furtherance of his commitment and devotion towards addressing the key challenges confronting his constituents and Kaduna State; on the 14th day of June 2019, two days after being sworn in, Senator Uba Sani unveiled his 5 – Point Action Plan for Kaduna Central Senatorial Zone as follows:

5 – Point Action Plan for Kaduna Central Senatorial Zone

1
Sustain strategic partnerships with critical Ministries, Departments and Agencies (MDAs) at the federal level in order to secure funding and support that benefit the people of Kaduna Central.
2
Strategically lobby to ensure the implementation of major and critical projects in Kaduna. These projects will be in line with the Kaduna State Infrastructure Master Plan (2018 - 2050) in order to guarantee synergy with the Kaduna State Government.
3
Take a critical look at the Report of the APC Committee on True Federalism and craft/sponsor Bill's that will help reposition the federating units and sustain Nigeria's unity.
4
Create an Open Forum for citizens of Kaduna Central Senatorial Zone. The Accountability Forum will be an avenue for critical engagement with my constituents in keeping with the dictates of participatory democracy and inclusive governance.
5
Ensure the passage of bills for the establishment of important tertiary and health institutions in Kaduna State.

At the unveiling ceremony, which was also attended by the Governor of Kaduna State, His Excellency Nasir El-Rufai, Senator Uba Sani revealed that for effective delivery of his mandate, he had established constituency offices in all the Local Government Areas under his constituency and accordingly appointed officers to oversee the respective offices. He also appointed an Administrative Officer, a Project Implementation Officer, an ICT Officer and a Director of Strategic Communication to make the offices function at an optimal level.

For effective administration, a Zonal Constituency Officer was appointed to oversee the activities of all the Local Government constituency offices, as well as supervise all other staff, and deliver reports to the Senator on a weekly basis. This way, the Senator is constantly informed of the goings-on challenges and needs of his constituents and can address them promptly and effectively.

The Zonal Constituency Office is located at No. 1 Ibrahim Biu Road, by IsahKaita Road, Kaduna State and is open between 8a.m to 6p.m from Monday to Saturday, in order to enable constituents to make reports, complaints, contributions, requests and suggestions on a regular basis. The Senator also makes the conscious effort to visit his Senatorial Zone frequently to interface with his constituents.

However, for constituents who are not presently residing in the Senatorial Zone or are in the diaspora, several other communication platforms were created to bridge the communication gap between Senator Uba Sani and his constituents.
These channels have so – far informed every single step which the Senator has taken since his assumption of office in June till today.
